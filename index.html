Pig Dice

Roll a dice rules


Each turn player repeatedly rolls a dice until either 1 is rolled or player decides to "hold".


  • If player rolls 1, they score nothing and it's next players turn
  • If the player rolls any other number it's added to their turn total and the players turn continues
  • If player chooses "hold" their tyrn total is added to their score and it's next player's turn

First player to score 100 points wins.
